# TurboFix — Marketing Video Script

This is a high-conversion, punchy video ad script (30-45 seconds) optimized for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and WhatsApp status broadcasts. It is specifically localized for factory owners and managers in the **Pune MIDC industrial belt** (Chakan, Bhosari, Pimpri-Chinchwad).

---

## 🎥 Video Overview
* **Format**: Vertical video (9:16 aspect ratio).
* **Tone**: Professional, urgent, ROI-focused, and highly visual.
* **Music**: High-energy, modern electronic beat with industrial synth highlights (building tempo).
* **Actors**: 1 Factory Owner/Manager (worried/frustrated, then relieved), 1 Worker (on the shop floor).

1. **Keep it Local**: Show real factory backdrops matching local sheet-metal, CNC, or injection-molding shops in Chakan/Bhosari to drive resonance.
2. **Rupee-Anchored Math**: In description copy accompanying the video, call out the math: *“For a typical Pune MIDC shop, reducing downtime by just 4 hours a month recovers up to ₹25,000 in lost margin.”*
3. **Instagram Target Parameters**:
   * **Location**: Drop pin on Chakan Industrial Area, Bhosari MIDC, and Pimpri-Chinchwad (radius 5-10km).
   * **Interests**: "Factory", "Manufacturing", "CNC Router", "Machine Shop", "Operations management".
