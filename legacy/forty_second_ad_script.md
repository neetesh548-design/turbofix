# TurboFix — 40-Second Marketing Video Script

This script is engineered to target the emotional and financial pain points of factory owners in India, directly comparing the current chaotic reality ("What is happening now") with the high-value automated future ("The value we provide").

---

## ⏱️ Timing Blueprint (Total: 40 Seconds)
* **0:00 - 0:10**: The Pain Area (Breakdown panic & search delay)
* **0:10 - 0:20**: What is Happening Now (Process leaks & margin drain)
* **0:20 - 0:32**: The Value We Provide (The WhatsApp QR + AI flow)
* **0:32 - 0:40**: The Business Payoff & Call to Action (70% faster MTTR)

## 💡 Production Details
* **Background Music**: Starts with an ominous, low industrial drone with a clock ticking sound (0:00-0:20). Swells into a high-tempo, clean corporate tech beat (0:20-0:40).
* **Vocalization**: The voiceover should be recorded by a voice artist speaking in clear, professional Indian English (or Hindi/Marathi depending on the localized run).
