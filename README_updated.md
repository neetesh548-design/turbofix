# TurboFix – AI Maintenance Decision Platform for Manufacturing SMEs

TurboFix is an AI-powered maintenance decision platform built specifically for small and medium manufacturing industries.

Unlike traditional CMMS solutions that primarily record maintenance activities, TurboFix helps maintenance teams make better decisions before machines fail. It combines machine history, preventive maintenance, AI, and operational intelligence into one simple platform.

## Vision

To become the AI operating system for maintenance in every small and medium factory by reducing downtime, improving maintenance planning, and enabling data-driven maintenance decisions.

## Why TurboFix?

Most SMEs still manage maintenance using Excel, paper registers, phone calls, and WhatsApp. This leads to:

- Reactive maintenance
- Unplanned machine breakdowns
- Poor maintenance visibility
- Lost production time
- Difficult shutdown planning
- Missing maintenance history
- Inefficient spare planning

TurboFix transforms maintenance from reactive to predictive and intelligent.

## Core Capabilities

### Machine Management

- Digital machine register
- QR-code based machine identification
- Machine specifications
- Service history
- Warranty tracking
- Photos and documents
- Manual management

### AI Maintenance Assistant

Technicians and maintenance heads can ask natural language questions such as:

- Which machines require attention today?
- Which machine is most likely to fail?
- Why is this motor overheating?
- Show me the manual for this machine.
- What spare parts are required?
- Which machine costs us the most?

### AI Shutdown Planner

One of the biggest challenges for maintenance teams is planning shutdowns.

Example:

> "We are taking a planned shutdown this Sunday. Which machines should be serviced?"

TurboFix automatically:

- Identifies overdue PM activities
- Finds machines becoming due before the next shutdown
- Prioritizes work by business risk
- Checks spare availability
- Estimates manpower
- Estimates maintenance hours
- Suggests maintenance sequence
- Generates work orders
- Produces an optimized shutdown plan
